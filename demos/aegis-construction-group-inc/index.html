Straight answers

Questions we get on every estimate.

How do I know if I need a new roof or just a repair?

Age, the state of the shingles, and whether the leak is in one place or several. We'll tell you which it is, in writing, and we don't sell replacements to people who need repairs.

How long does a roof take?

Most single-family tear-offs are one to two days. Bigger or flat roofs run longer. We'll give you the schedule before we start and call you if weather moves it.

Do you work with insurance?

Yes. We document damage with photos and a written scope so your adjuster has what they need.

Can you work in winter?

Emergency repairs year-round. Full replacements are weather-dependent — we'll be honest about whether yours can wait for spring.

Get a written estimate this week.

Free written estimate, usually within 48 hours. No pressure, no sales visit — just a number and an honest opinion.
